---
title: "Configuring The Solution Configurations"
---

This section has information on how to configure Salesforce objects to Content Types through the Solution Configurations section within Hyland for Salesforce.

As a system administrator, you can configure different standard and custom Salesforce objects and associate them with Content Types you created. These objects are needed to capture supported files and store them as documents to the Hyland Content Innovation Cloud. You can also create configurations to view captured documents.

Before proceeding to make configuration changes, ensure you complete the settings under the Application Configurations tab. For detailed instructions, see Configuring the Application Configurations.
