This section contains information on how to uninstall the Hyland for Salesforce.

To uninstall Hyland for Salesforce, you must remove the Document List View component or  hfsRecordDLPage from the record detail pages of any Salesforce objects configured during installation and uninstall the Hyland for Salesforce package.
