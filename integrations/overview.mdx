## How Integrations Work

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Connector">
    Select the integration that matches your enterprise system from the sidebar. Each connector includes installation, configuration, and usage guides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Install & Configure">
    Follow the connector-specific installation steps to deploy and authenticate the integration in your environment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable Content Ingestion">
    Configure content sources and ingestion schedules to pull documents from your external system into CIC automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Surface Knowledge Discovery">
    Launch Knowledge Discovery from within your connected application to let users ask questions against ingested content.
  </Step>
</Steps>
