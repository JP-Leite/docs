## Checking for Updates

Update information for the Hyland Content Intelligence app is available directly within Confluence. When a new version of the app is published, a notification and an **Update** button appear in the Atlassian app management panel.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open App Management">
    In Confluence, navigate to **Apps → Manage Apps** from the main navigation menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate Hyland Content Intelligence">
    Scroll to or search for **Hyland Content Intelligence** in the installed apps list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Update">
    If an update is available, the app entry will show the new version number alongside an **Update** button. Click **Update** to review release notes before proceeding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Apply the Update">
    Click the **Update** button to install the new version. Atlassian will apply the update in the background — ingestion will resume automatically once the update completes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Update Behaviour

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Configuration Preserved" icon="gear">
    Your HxI credentials, API token, and Source ID are preserved across updates. You do not need to re-enter configuration after applying an update.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Minimal Downtime" icon="clock">
    Updates are applied by Atlassian in the background. There is typically a brief interruption to Auto Ingestion during the update, but no pages are lost.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Info>
  Atlassian may also apply **automatic updates** for minor patch releases. Check your Atlassian organisation's app update policy if you require manual approval for all updates.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="The Update button is not visible">
  If no update is available, the **Update** button will not appear. Your installed version is current. Check the Atlassian Marketplace listing for the latest version and compare it with the version shown in **Manage Apps**.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Ingestion stopped after an update">
  Navigate to **Apps → Hyland Content Intelligence → Settings** and confirm your credentials are still valid. In rare cases, a major update may require you to re-authorise the connector by re-saving your configuration.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I need to roll back to a previous version">
  Atlassian does not support direct version rollback through the app management UI. Contact Hyland Support if you need assistance with a version-specific issue.
</Accordion>
