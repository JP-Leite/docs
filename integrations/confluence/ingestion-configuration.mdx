---
title: "Ingestion Configuration"
description: "Supply your Hyland Content Intelligence environment credentials to connect the Confluence Connector."
---

The Ingestion Configuration section links the Confluence Connector to your **Hyland Insights (HxI)** environment. You must complete all four fields before content ingestion can begin.

---

## Configuration Fields

<Steps>
  <Step title="Environment Details">
    Provide the **Environment ID** and **Client ID** for your HxI environment. These identifiers are unique to your Hyland tenant and can be obtained from your Hyland administrator or the HxI portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Security Credentials">
    Enter the **Client Secret** associated with your HxI environment. Treat this value like a password — never store it in Confluence pages or share it externally.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Source Identification">
    Provide the **Source ID** (UUID) that identifies which Content Intelligence agent or source this connector feeds. Your Hyland administrator will provide this value when provisioning the integration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Field Reference

| Field | Description | Example Format |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Environment ID** | Unique identifier for your HxI environment | `env-xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx` |
| **Source ID** | UUID identifying which CIC agent uses this connector | `xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx` |
| **Client ID** | OAuth2 client identifier for the HxI environment | `client-xxxxxxxx` |
| **Client Secret** | OAuth2 secret for authenticating to HxI | *(do not record in plain text)* |

<Info>
  All four fields are **required**. Saving the configuration with any field blank will result in a validation error and the connector will be unable to ingest content.
</Info>

---

## Troubleshooting

<Accordion title="Validation fails after saving credentials">
  Double-check that the **Environment ID**, **Client ID**, and **Client Secret** were copied from the HxI portal exactly, with no leading or trailing spaces. Also confirm that the **Source ID** UUID matches the source configured in your Hyland Content Intelligence environment.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="I don't know my Environment ID or Source ID">
  These values are provisioned by your Hyland administrator. Open a support ticket with Hyland or contact your Hyland Customer Success Manager to retrieve them.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="My Client Secret has been rotated">
  Update the **Client Secret** field in the connector settings with the new secret and save. No reinstallation of the app is required.
</Accordion>
