## What is the Confluence Connector?

The Confluence Connector is a Hyland-built Atlassian app that bridges your organisation's structured and unstructured Confluence content with the **Hyland Knowledge Discovery** module inside Content Intelligence Cloud.

Once installed and configured, the connector continuously feeds Confluence pages and blog posts into Content Intelligence — enabling your team to:

- Ask natural language questions against your Confluence knowledge base
- Invoke Knowledge Discovery AI agents that reason over live Confluence content
- Automate ingestion so CIC always reflects your latest documentation

<Info>
  At this time, only **Confluence Pages** and **Blog Posts** can be ingested. Attachments, Jira links, and whiteboards are not yet supported.
</Info>

---

## How it Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the Hyland App">
    A site administrator installs the **Hyland Content Intelligence** app from the Atlassian Marketplace using an installation link provided by your Hyland representative.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the Connector">
    Supply your HxI Environment ID, Client ID, Client Secret, and Atlassian API token in the connector settings panel.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ingest Content">
    Choose **Bulk Ingestion** for an initial load of existing pages, then enable **Auto Ingestion** to keep content in sync as it changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Query with AI">
    Use Hyland Knowledge Discovery agents to search, summarise, and reason over your Confluence content from within CIC.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Capabilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Auto Ingestion" icon="rotate" href="/integrations/confluence/auto-ingestion">
    Content is automatically pushed to CIC every time a Confluence page or blog is updated — no manual intervention required.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Bulk Ingestion" icon="bolt" href="/integrations/confluence/bulk-ingestion">
    Load large volumes of existing Confluence content into CIC in a single operation — ideal for initial onboarding.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Secure Authentication" icon="shield-halved" href="/integrations/confluence/atlassian-api-token-configuration">
    Authentication is handled via Atlassian REST API tokens scoped to a specific user, keeping credentials isolated and auditable.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Simple Configuration" icon="gear" href="/integrations/confluence/configuring-confluence-connector">
    A guided settings panel inside the Atlassian app walks you through every required field in minutes.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
