section: "Hyland for Guidewire PolicyCenter"
---

Hyland for Guidewire PolicyCenter streamlines insurance processes and improves access to information, while automating previously manual tasks. For example, a user of an ECM repository (such as the Content Innovation Cloud) could capture and store insurance policy-related documents in the CIC repository electronically, linking them to the associated policy's data within Guidewire. Insurance personnel can instantly access these documents directly from Guidewire, improving decision-making and eliminating the need to search through multiple applications, file shares, or paper records. Guidewire PolicyCenter users can also upload documents into an ECM repository for long-term storage without needing to leave the PolicyCenter application they are familiar with.

With Hyland for Guidewire PolicyCenter, insurance adjusters have instant access to the information they need to issue and administer insurance policies. Documents such as policy endorsements, coverage letters, and inspection reports are only a click away, thereby accelerating the policy issuance process, reducing bottlenecks, and facilitating timely decisions.
