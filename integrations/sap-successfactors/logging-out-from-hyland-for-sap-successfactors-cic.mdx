---
title: "Logging Out From Hyland For Sap Successfactors Cic"
---

This section contains information on how to log out from the application.

Note: Ensure you are logged on to Hyland for SAP SuccessFactors CIC and third-party cookies are allowed in your browser's settings.
To log out from the application:

1.  From the top right corner of the screen, click the round icon with a letter in it.
2.  From the drop-down list, click Sign Out. The user session is ended.
