title: "Knowledge Discovery Integration"
description: "Enable natural language search and AI-powered insights directly within your OnBase clients."

Hyland Knowledge Discovery allows users to ask natural language questions and receive AI-generated answers based on content ingested from your OnBase repository.

## How to Use Knowledge Discovery

The Knowledge Discovery interface is intuitive and focused on conversational retrieval.

1.  **Select an Agent**: Choose the AI agent that has been configured for your specific document set.
2.  **Ask your Question**: Type a natural language query (e.g., "What are the payment terms for the Acme Corp contract?").
3.  **Review the Response**: The agent provides a synthesized answer.
4.  **Verify Sources**: Click on any **referenced document** citation to open a preview of the source file and verify the information.
5.  **Regenerate**: If the answer isn't precise enough, use the **Regenerate** button to get an alternative response.

---

## Configuring the Integration

Before users can access Knowledge Discovery from OnBase, an administrator must configure the connection in the **OnBase Administration Portal**.

1.  **Obtain the URL**: Identify your Knowledge Discovery URL. It will include your Environment ID (e.g., `https://<EnvironmentID>.insight.ncp.hyland.com/discovery/`).
2.  **System Settings**: In the OnBase Administration Portal, navigate to **System Settings**.
3.  **Enable Feature**: Scroll to the **Knowledge Discovery** section and toggle **Enable Ask Discovery Buttons**.
4.  **Enter Connection Info**:
    - **Knowledge Discovery Base URL**: Paste the URL obtained in step 1.
    - **Knowledge Discovery Environment ID**: Enter your unique Environment ID.
5.  **Save**: Click **Save** to apply the settings.

---

## Launching from OnBase Clients

Once configured, the "Ask Discovery" icon will appear in the following OnBase interfaces:

- **OnBase Web Client**: Accessible via the browser-based interface.
- **OnBase Unity Client**: Available directly within the desktop application.
- **OnBase App Builder**: Can be integrated into custom applications built with App Builder.
- **Contextual Search**: When viewing a document, you can launch Knowledge Discovery to ask questions specifically about that file.
