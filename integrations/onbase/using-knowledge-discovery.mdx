Hyland Knowledge Discovery allows users to ask natural language questions and receive comprehensive, AI-generated answers based on content ingested from OnBase.

<Info>
  For complete Knowledge Discovery documentation, see the [Hyland Knowledge Discovery documentation](https://support.hyland.com/access?dita:topicPath=lkq1732566025369.dita).
</Info>

---

## How to Use Knowledge Discovery

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose an Agent">
    Select the agent that best matches your query. Hover over an agent avatar to see a short summary of its capabilities.

    **Tip:** For information on creating an agent, see *Creating an Agent* in the Hyland Knowledge Discovery documentation.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Ask Your Question">
    Type your question in the input field. Provide as much detail as possible to get the most accurate response.

    Once you've selected files or a knowledge source, the button changes from **Ask Discovery** to **Ask**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit the Query">
    Click **Ask** to submit your question. The AI Agent uses the selected content to generate a response.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the Response">
    Once the AI response appears, you can:

    - **Regenerate** the response to get an alternative answer.
    - **Copy** the response to the clipboard.
    - **View referenced documents** — click any referenced document to open it in the preview screen.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Handle Conversation Discards">
    If you switch to a new AI conversation, a warning will appear stating **"This conversation will be discarded."**

    - Select **Okay** to discard and start a new conversation.
    - Select **Cancel** to return to the current conversation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
