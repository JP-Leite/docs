title: "Documents Ingestion"
description: "Monitor, manually trigger, and retry document ingestion from OnBase to the content lake."

Documents are ingested from OnBase to the content lake automatically by the OnBase Connector for Content Intelligence. You can monitor ingestion status, trigger manual ingestion, or retry failed ingestions.
