Automate is a low-code application development platform available as a service on the Content Innovation Cloud™. It has been designed for organizations to quickly and easily create intelligent solutions that orchestrate and streamline from simple to complex content-centric business processes. It is meant to serve multiple business domains and its cloud-native architecture offers maximum availability and dynamic scaling.

The Studio Modeler application is a low-code tool for configuring Content and Process models. It enables you to model your data and to orchestrate the journey from concept to archiving and deleting, and all the transactions and transformations between.

## Capabilities

Studio Modeler enables Solution Builders to:
- **Define data structures**, metadata properties, and reusable schemas from web forms.
- **Construct BPMN 2.0 compliant** process models using a drag-and-drop web interface.
- **Integrate** with content and external systems via REST API and dedicated connectors.
