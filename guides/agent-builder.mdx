---
title: "Agent Builder"
description: "Create, specialize, and monitor AI agents at scale."
---

Agent Builder is an agent configuration and lifecycle management tool. It enables organizations to use AI agents to perform specific content-driven tasks or specialize in particular areas of the business, augmenting the human workforce and enabling organization to implement AI at scale.

- **Create and manage** agentic AIs to automatically execute complex workflows and tasks.
- **Specialize** created AI agents to better suit their appointed purpose.
- **Monitor** AI agent performance and diminish potential automation problems for critical tasks while maintaining low human presence for general tasks.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access the Application">
    Learn how to [Access the Agent Builder App](/content-intelligence/agent-builder/accessing-the-agent-builder-app) or [via Studio Modeler](/content-intelligence/agent-builder/accessing-the-agent-builder-in-studio-modeler).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create an Agent">
    Define the agent's purpose, configure the large language model (LLM), and define input properties.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test and Deploy">
    Use the built-in hot-reload environment to test your agent before releasing it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
