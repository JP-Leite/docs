Hyland Governance provides a governance framework that supports both on-premises and cloud-based repositories, enabling organisations to manage compliance without relocating content. It ensures data integrity, reduces risk, and provides central oversight across multiple repositories.

## Key Capabilities

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Governance Project">
    Define a governance project in Studio Modeler to establish the scope and structure of your compliance framework.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build a File Plan">
    Create a file plan with categories, retention schedules, and disposition rules that align with your regulatory requirements.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Retention Policies">
    Assign retention policies to categories to control how long records are kept and when they are disposed of.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Deploy and Monitor">
    Deploy your governance project to activate retention enforcement across your CIC repositories.
  </Step>
</Steps>
