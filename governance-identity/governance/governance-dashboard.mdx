Governance Dashboard is a tool that provides you an instant view of the key issues in Governance. It allows you to quickly identify records missing required data, monitor legal hold statuses across all records, display the status of ongoing review workflows, help managers follow up on pending approvals, and keep processes moving. Additionally, you can visualize record volumes by category and retention status to plan for storage, migration, or disposal needs.

You can view records that have not been accessed in over five years to evaluate archival or disposal options and optimize storage. Retention schedules across departments are monitored to ensure consistent policy application and prevent records from being kept longer than necessary.

The following list shows available dashboards:

-  Record Health
-  Active Retention Status
-  Incomplete Record
-  Cutoff Tracker
-  Disposition Tracker
