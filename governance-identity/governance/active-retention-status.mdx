---
title: "Active Retention Status"
---

The Active Retention Status dashboard displays the number of records across categories with the status Under Retention.The entire bar indicates the total number of records in a specific category. The colored part of the bar indicates the number of records with Under Retention status within this category.

Use the following options to sort and explore category details in the dashboard:

-  Sort the records by the number of records in the category. In the upper-right corner of the dashboard, select:
-  High to low: To sort the records starting from the highest number of the records in the category.
-  Low to high: To sort the records starting from the lowest number of the records in the category.
-  Select action as needed. The following options are available:
| Action | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Hover over a grey part of the bar | Displays the total number of records for the category. |
| Hover over the colored part of the bar | Displays the number and percentage of records with Under Retention status in this category. |
| Click a column in the chart | Displays results on the Records Management tab, filtered by the Under Retention status and the category associated with that column. |
