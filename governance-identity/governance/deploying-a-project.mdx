---
title: "Deploying A Project"
---

When released projects are deployed they become end-user apps in Studio Administration. Deployment options are available from the Devops menu in Studio Administration.

For more information, see the section on deploying a project in the Hyland Intelligent Document Processing (IDP) documentation: [Deploying a Project](https://support.hyland.com/access?dita:topicPath=znk1745947949681.dita) . The Manager Access, User Access, and Connectors settings do not apply to Governance projects.
