Legal Hold Management can be accessed through the Governance tab in Workspace. The list of all Legal Hold Cases is stored there.

To access Legal Hold Management in Workspace:

1.  In Studio Admin, expand Process Admin on the left pane.
2.  Click Applications list to see the released applications.
3.  Find your project and click the corresponding link in the Digital workspace column. Workspace opens in a new tab in your browser.
4.  Click the  Governance  tab on the left pane.
5.  Click the Legal Hold Management tab. The Legal Hold Management interface is displayed.
You can create a legal hold in the Legal Hold Management interface. For information on creating a legal hold, see Creating a Legal Hold Case.
