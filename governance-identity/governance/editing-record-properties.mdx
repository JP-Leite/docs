A Governance Record Properties option allows for editing cutoff details of the records in Workspace. A Cutoff Date designates when the retention starts. The editing option is available only for records that haven't started the retention yet. As a result, this can be changed only for the records with an Incomplete or Ready status.

To edit a record in Workspace:

1.  Access a Governance tab in Workspace.
2.  Filter the records. For more information on filtering records, see Filtering Records.
3.  Select the record you want to edit.
4.  Click View Property. The Properties pane is displayed on the right side.
5.  Click Edit.
6.  Click on the calendar icon and select a date.
7.  Click OK.
8.  Click Save.
