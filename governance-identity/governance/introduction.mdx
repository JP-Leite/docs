title: "Introduction"
---

Hyland Governance is a cloud-native solution for compliance and secure litigation content management.

Hyland Governance provides a governance framework that supports both on-premises and cloud-based repositories. It has been designed to manage compliance without relocating content, ensuring data integrity and reducing risks associated with data relocation.

Organizations with multiple repositories can manage data independently by local autonomy while maintaining central oversight. This enables traceability of records across multiple repositories, enhances risk management, and allows for secure data-sharing protocols.

Designed for large volumes of data, Hyland Governance serves as a focal point for governance innovation.
