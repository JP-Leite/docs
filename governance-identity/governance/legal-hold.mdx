title: "Legal Hold Management"
---

Legal holds allow you to secure records that may be required for litigation or investigations, preventing their accidental modification or deletion even if they have reached their retention disposition.

## Managing Legal Hold Cases

A legal hold case acts as a container for records that must be preserved.

### Creating a Case
1. In the **Legal Hold Management** interface in Workspace, click **Create Legal Hold Case**.
2. Enter a unique **Name** (up to 64 characters).
3. Provide a **Reason** for the hold (up to 512 characters).
4. Click **Create Legal Hold Case**.

### Editing a Case
You can update the name, reason, or description of an existing case by selecting it and clicking the **Edit** button.

---

## Adding Records to a Hold

A record with any status (Ready, Under Retention, etc.) can be placed on hold.

1. Use the **Governance Filters** in Workspace to find the relevant records.
2. Select one or more records.
3. Click the **Add selected records to a legal hold** icon.
4. Select the target case(s) and click **Add Records to Selection**.

### Impact on Lifecycle
- **Status Change**: The record's visible status changes to **On Hold**.
- **Preservation**: The original retention lifecycle continues in the background (e.g., a record can move from Ready to Under Retention while on hold), but the record **cannot be deleted** until the hold is removed.

---

## Viewing and Monitoring Holds

In the **Legal Hold Management** tab, you can view a summary of all cases:
- **Reason for Hold**: Why the case was initiated.
- **Total Records Held**: Click this number to see the specific list of records assigned to that case.
- **Creation Details**: Who created the case and when.

## Verifying Integrity: Hyland Credentials

For stronger proof of compliance, Hyland uses cryptographic hashing to verify that content on hold has not been tampered with.

### Credential Status Icons
In the Records Management list, these icons indicate the status of the cryptographic hash:
- **(No Icon)**: Record is not currently on hold.
- **Pending Icon**: The record is queued for hashing.
- **Verified Icon**: The credential hash has been successfully generated and verified.
