Hyland Governance is a cloud-native solution for compliance and secure litigation content management.

Providing a governance framework that supports both on-premises and cloud-based repositories, Hyland Governance has been designed to manage compliance without relocating content, ensuring data integrity and reducing risks associated with data relocation.

With the Hyland Governance REST API, you can automate and streamline your management of content from multiple repositories independently by local autonomy, while maintaining central oversight. This enables traceability of records across multiple repositories, enhances risk management, and secures data-sharing protocols.

For more information on how administrators and users can perform a variety of compliance and content management tasks in the Studio Modeler and Workspace applications, see the [Hyland Governance](https://support.hyland.com/access?dita:topicPath=cfh1748506599447.dita) user documentation.
