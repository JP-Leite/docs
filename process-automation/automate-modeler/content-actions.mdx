---
title: "Content Actions"
---

The content connector is used to execute actions against the Content repository. The actions available involve creating, selecting, updating and managing content.

All of the actions that the content connector can execute as part of a service task can also be used as trigger actions. The properties are the same as they are when the action is attached to a service task.

The following actions are available:

-  CREATE_CONTENT
-  DELETE_CONTENT
-  FETCH_CONTENT
-  UPDATE_METADATA
-  UPDATE_FILE
-  MOVE_CONTENT
-  ADD_PERMISSION
-  REMOVE_PERMISSION
-  READ_FILE
-  SET_CONTENT_TYPE
