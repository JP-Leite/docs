The ACS connector is used to establish connection with Alfresco Content Services and use their functions on contents of the ACS repository, including downloading files to the Hyland repository.

The ACS connector appears on the process diagram as a rectangle with an Alfresco Content Services logo.

The authentication used for the ACS connector can be selected from the drop-down menu. All types of authentications are available. For more details, see Authentication.

The following actions are possible with the ACS connector:

-  EXECUTE_ACTION
-  ADD_SECURITY_MARKS
-  GET_SECURITY_MARKS
-  DECLARE_RECORD
-  SET_PERMISSIONS
-  REMOVE_ASPECT
-  ADD_ASPECT
-  UNLOCK_NODE
-  LOCK_NODE
-  UPDATE_TAG
-  DELETE_FOLDER
-  DELETE_FILE
-  MOVE_FOLDER
-  MOVE_FILE
-  COPY_FOLDER
-  COPY_FILE
-  UPDATE_FILE_CONTENT
-  READ_FILE_CONTENT
-  UPDATE_METADATA
-  SELECT_METADATA
-  SET_TYPE
-  CREATE_FOLDER
-  CREATE_FILE
-  SELECT_FOLDER
-  SELECT_FILE
-  DOWNLOAD_FILE
