The Content Types section allows you to create and define content types that will be used within your application.

Content types are a set of properties that define content. You can use the system content types to take advantage of the default metadata and properties, or compose your own content type of custom-made schemas and mixins, which allow you to create detailed, tailor-made solutions content type with the exact properties to fit your process.

Within the platform, a content type may be defined by the list of its schemas and mixins, its lifecycle, the forms to present to the user, or its versioning policy
