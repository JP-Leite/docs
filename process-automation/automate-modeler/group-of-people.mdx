---
title: "Group Of People"
---

Group of people fields allow form fillers to select a single or multiple groups from the list of groups available in the application.

Group of people fields belong to the People field group.

The Group of people fields do not have the Placeholder property.

The advanced properties for a Group of people field are:

| Property | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Mode | Determines whether only a single, or multiple groups can be selected. |

Note:
You can use the Edit Visibility Conditions button to decide when the field is visible or hidden.
