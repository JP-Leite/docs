---
title: "Deleting Connectors From The Project"
---

You can delete a connector from your project. After deleting, it can no longer be used in your processes.

To delete a connector:

1.  Open the Studio Modeler project.
2.  From the left-hand pane, select the Connectors drop-down menu.
3.  Select the connector that you want to delete. The connector is opened as a new tab.
4.  From the top bar, select vertical ellipsis button >  Delete. A confirmation window displayed.
5.  Click Confirm to delete the process.

The process is deleted.
