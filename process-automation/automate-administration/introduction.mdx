title: "Introduction"
---

Automate is a low-code application development platform available as a service on the Content Innovation Cloud™. It has been designed for organizations to quickly and easily create intelligent solutions that orchestrate and streamline from simple to complex content-centric business processes. It is meant to serve multiple business domains and its cloud-native architecture offers maximum availability and dynamic scaling.

It is composed of three main components: ​

- The Modeler application, where professional developers and non-technical power users create, update, and release applications.
- The Admin application, where administrators deploy applications, manage user permissions, and monitor deployed applications.
- The Workspace, where end-users can start new content-centric processes, and manage their work.

This manual is dedicated solely to the Administration application. Additionally, it gives further information on the Automate architecture to provide better understanding of its concepts.
