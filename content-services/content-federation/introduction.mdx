---
title: "Introduction"
---

The Content Federation Services (CFS) allows connectivity from Content Innovation Cloud (CIC) components into content repositories installed within the customer data center. The CFS does this without modifications to existing network implementation in the customer’s data center. The CFS allows users to access content stored in their repositories from CIC applications, configure secure connections to their repositories, and leverage features available in the CIC.

The Content Innovation Cloud (CIC) is an all-encompassing cloud services platform that allows your organization to create and manage personalized digital experiences for your customers, employees, and partners. Combining the best of advanced technologies, such as machine learning and artificial intelligence, and a modern user interface, the CIC offers a comprehensive suite of app-building tools and APIs that empower you to customize your cloud processes, content management, reporting, and integrations.
