The Content Federation Services (CFS) application allows users to generate tokens that are used by the CF Broker to connect to the CF Gateway. Users may also check the status of existing tokens and delete tokens on the Token Management tab. The Token Management tab is part of the Content Federation Admin Portal.

The following topics describe how to perform actions on the Token Management tab:

-  Token Management
-  Generating Broker Tokens
-  Deleting Tokens
