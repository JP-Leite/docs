For OnBase repositories, Hyland Identity Service (IdS) 5.1.0 (or later) is required to authenticate and authorize requests from the Content Innovation Cloud (CIC) to OnBase through the Content Federation Services (CFS).

For more information on how to install and configure Hyland IdS, see the Identity and Access Management Services documentation for the version of Hyland IdS you are using.
