In the Hyland Capture Scan and Import interface, you can perform a batch capture process to capture image or PDF documents and prepare them for one of the following:

-
Later verification in the Hyland Capture Verify Data interface

-
Direct export out of the Hyland Capture application

While preparing to capture documents, you can also add, edit, or delete the input sources available for the batch capture process if you want to change your scanning devices or sweep directories from which to import documents.
