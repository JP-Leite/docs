---
title: "Selecting A Content Queue"
---

A Content Queue is a representation of batches grouped together by a similar business function. For example, a content queue for HR would have all HR related documents (Resumes, Sick Leave, Performance Reviews). They have associated classes (document types) that are specific to the content queue and represent the purpose of the batches being processed.

You can view a list of the available Content Queues in the Queue List pane on the left side of the batch list page. You can select the relevant Content Queue that you want to work with. When you select a content queue, the batches mapped to the selected content queue are displayed.

To select a content queue, select the relevant Content Queue from the Queue List drop-down list.
