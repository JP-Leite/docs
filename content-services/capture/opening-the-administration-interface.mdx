---
title: "Opening The Administration Interface"
---

While working in the Hyland Experience Capture application, you can switch to the Administration interface from the Scan and Import or Verify Data interfaces as needed. You can also open the Hyland Experience Capture Administration interface from the Hyland Experience Administration Portal.

Note: You need to have a valid subscription to access the application.
To switch to the Administration interface:

1.  Open the Hyland Experience Capture application and log in (see Opening the Hyland Capture Application). Alternatively, navigate to the Hyland Experience Administration Portal.
2.  Open the Administration interface.
-
If you opened the Hyland Experience Capture application, click the Administration Configuration button in the upper-right corner of the application.

-
If you want to access the Administration interface from the Hyland Experience Administration Portal, select the appropriate environment, and click Hyland Experience Capture Config.

3.  The Administration interface is displayed in a new tab.
