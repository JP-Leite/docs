---
title: "Currency Handling And Validation"
---

While performing extraction verification of documents with a currency field, any number entered in the currency amount field is auto-formatted according to the validation rules in the following sections.

When considering currency validation, also note the following:

-
Only U.S. currency formats are supported.

-
Whole numbers (without commas or decimals) are always valid. When these values are exported from the Hyland Capture application, .00 is automatically appended to them.
