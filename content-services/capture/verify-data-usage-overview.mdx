The Hyland Experience Capture Verify Data interface enables you to validate and correct automatically processed incoming documents. You can perform manual classification of documents, correction of automatic classification and extraction results, and manual indexing of documents.

An intuitively structured user interface, sophisticated status management and filter techniques make this application easy-to-use and display only those documents that you need to check (currently, only PNG image documents are supported).

Keyboard shortcuts are available for most operations, enabling you to perform your tasks quickly. Through automatic locking, teams of users can safely process document batches.
