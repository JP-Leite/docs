When a user opens a batch for the first time, the Owner field displays the name of the user. Unless the batch owner submits the batch or clicks Return to Batches, other users will not be able to open the batch and will receive the following error message: Batch [batch name] is locked by [name of owner]. However, batches that are locked by other users are not displayed when the Show batches to be verified check box is selected.

When an owner of a batch opens a batch and logs out of the application, without submitting it, the batch is unlocked and is available to other users for editing. However, if a batch owner exits the application abruptly by closing it, or it closes due to server outage, power outage or some other abnormal conditions, the batch remains in a locked state and is not available to other users for editing.
