You can reorder pages in and across documents using the thumbnail images. You can drag a thumbnail image from its current position and drop it to its new position. You can drag and drop multiple pages. When you drag and drop a page, a blue visual indicator appears to assist you with the new drop position.

To reorder pages:

1.  On the relevant document area, do one of the following:
-
To select a single page, click the thumbnail image of the page that you want to move.

-
To select multiple pages, click the thumbnail image and select the check box in the upper-left corner of each thumbnail image. A tick appears on the selected pages.

You can also select multiple pages in a range by clicking on a page, pressing and holding Shift, and clicking on the page that marks the end of the range. You can then press Ctrl and click the relevant page to cancel the selection.

2.  Drag and drop the thumbnail images to their new position by placing the page within the visual drop indicator boundary. The corresponding document is updated and the new sequence is saved.  Note:
You can also reorder pages by using common keyboard shortcuts, such as Ctrl + X for cut and Ctrl + V for paste.
