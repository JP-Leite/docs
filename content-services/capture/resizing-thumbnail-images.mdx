You can increase or decrease the size of the thumbnail images to optimize the view so that you can quickly work with the document, reorder the pages, split or append the document or perform some other action.

To resize thumbnail images, do one of the following:

-
Press Alt and + on the keyboard, to increase the size of the thumbnail images.

Note:
You can increase the size of the thumbnail images by two times their original size.

-
Press Alt and - on the keyboard, to reduce the size of the thumbnail images.

Note:
You can reduce the size of the thumbnail images to half of their original size.
