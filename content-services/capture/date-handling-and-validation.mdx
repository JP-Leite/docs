The following criteria must be followed while performing extraction verification of documents with a date field.

Automated date conversion: For numeric dates either entered, or pasted from the document (using rubber banding or selecting), you need to convert all dates into the MM/DD/YYYY format. The acceptable input formats with segmentation characters being slash (/), period (.), dash (-), are as follows:

-
M/D/YY, MM/D/YY, MM/DD/YY, MM/DD/YYYY

-
YY/M/D, YY/M/DD, YY/MM/DDYYYY/MM/DD

-
D/M/YY, DD/M/YY, DD/MM/YY, DD/MM/YYYY

-
Ambiguous dates, where it could be MM/DD or DD/MM need to be assumed as being MM/DD.

The acceptable input formats without any segmentation characters are as follows:

-
A minimum of 6 digits is required

-
MMDDYY, MMDDYYYY

-
DDMMYY, DDMMYYYY

-
YYMMDD, YYYYMMDD

-
Ambiguous dates, where it could be MMDD or DDMM need to be assumed as being MMDD.
