---
title: "Verifying Document Metadata"
---

You can verify or review document metadata with the Snippet Viewer on the Document Viewer.

For more information, see Verifying Document Metadata with the Snippet Viewer.

For verification of extracted results in documents that contain a date field, see Date Handling and Validation.

For verification of extracted results in documents containing currency fields, see Currency Handling and Validation.

To verify document metadata:

1.  Navigate to the relevant field which you want to verify and do one of the following:
-
On the Document Viewer, double-click the relevant text which you want to map to the corresponding metadata field.

-
Draw a rectangle around the text that you want to map to the metadata field.

2.  Type the text that you want to map and then select it.  Note:
When you begin typing, the auto-completion feature prompts and suggests the best match among all of the words or candidates available. The auto-selected text also appears highlighted on the Document Viewer. However, multiple words are not supported during auto completion in extraction verification. To override auto-completion, continue typing the desired text or rubber band the whole word.

3.  Defocus the field by clicking outside it or pressing Enter or Tab. The field data is saved.
