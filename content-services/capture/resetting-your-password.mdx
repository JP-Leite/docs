---
title: "Resetting Your Password"
---

When you click Forgot Password in the Hyland Capture application's login dialog box, you are prompted to confirm your email address.

To reset your password:

1.  Confirm or update your Hyland Experience email address in the field provided and click Submit. An email with a link to reset your password is sent to the address you provide.
2.  In the email sent from the Hyland Experience platform, click the reset password link. The reset password page is displayed.
3.  Enter your new password in the New Password field.
4.  Enter the password again in the Confirm New Password field.
5.  Click Reset. A confirmation message is displayed. For information on logging in to the Hyland Capture application with your new password, see Opening the Hyland Capture Application.
