Once you have captured documents into your Hyland Capture system, you can review and organize these documents in the document review layout. You can zoom, scale, and rotate each page of the captured documents in the document viewer on the left side of the layout. You can also append, split, delete, and classify the captured documents in the thumbnail viewer to the right of the document viewer.

If necessary, you can scan additional documents into a pending, scanned batch without having to return to the document capture layout.

If you captured documents in a Content Queue that has been configured to bypass the Hyland Capture Verify Data interface, you can also index the batch using the Metadata Panel on the right side of the document review layout. This prepares the batch for direct export out of the application. Otherwise, the batch can be indexed later once it is sent to the Verify Data interface. In either case, you can submit the batch when you are ready to process it out of the document review layout.

When preparing a batch for verification or export, also note the following:

- Once the document review layout is displayed after you start capturing batch documents, you cannot return to the document capture layout until you submit or delete the pending batch.
- You must maintain a successful connection to the Capture Desktop service, or the service on which the Desktop Host runs, to perform actions on documents and pages in the captured batch. If you lose your connection while performing an action, an error message is displayed.
