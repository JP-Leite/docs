## What is Content Innovation Cloud?

The **Content Innovation Cloud (CIC)** is Hyland's all-encompassing cloud services platform that enables organisations to create, manage, and deliver personalised digital experiences for customers, employees, and partners.

CIC combines modern content management with advanced AI and machine learning capabilities, giving your team:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Content Services" icon="folder-open">
    A secure, scalable cloud repository for storing and governing your documents, with full version control, permissions, and metadata management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Process Automation" icon="gears">
    Low-code and no-code tools to automate content-driven business processes via Studio Modeler and the Automate process engine.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content Intelligence" icon="brain">
    AI-powered extraction, classification, and understanding of content — turning unstructured documents into structured, searchable data.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Discovery" icon="magnifying-glass">
    Contextual AI agents that search and synthesise information from your enterprise content to answer business questions instantly.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## The CIC Ecosystem

The Content Innovation Cloud ships with a suite of ready-to-use applications addressing different business needs. These applications are designed for different personas within your organisation.

### For Administrators & Business Users

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Administration Portal" icon="shield-halved">
    **Role:** Business/System Administrator
    Manage subscriptions, applications, and users across your CIC customer account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Studio Administration" icon="server">
    **Role:** System Administrator
    Administer and monitor Studio releases, deployed applications, and running process instances.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Workspace" icon="window-restore">
    **Role:** Business User
    A modern UI to navigate the repository, manage content-based processes, and collaborate.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Discovery" icon="brain">
    **Role:** Data Analyst
    AI agents that reason over your document repository to answer complex business questions.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credentials" icon="id-card">
    **Role:** Business User
    Design, issue, and deliver verifiable credentials with the Hyland Credentials framework.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hyland for Workday" icon="briefcase">
    **Role:** Business User
    Capture and process Hyland content directly from within your Workday application.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### For Developers & Solution Builders

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Studio Modeler" icon="draw-polygon">
    **Role:** App Builder
    A visual, drag-and-drop environment to model and release content-based applications.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content REST API" icon="code">
    **Role:** Developer
    Build custom applications on top of the CIC repository using the comprehensive REST API.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Credentials REST API" icon="key">
    **Role:** Developer
    Programmatically generate and manage verifiable credentials.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
