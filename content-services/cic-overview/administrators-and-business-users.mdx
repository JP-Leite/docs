Administrators and business users can leverage the following CIC applications to serve their business needs. Roles in this context include business users, business administrators, system administrators, and data analysts.

---

## Available Applications

| Application | Business Role | Description |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Administration Portal** | Business Administrator, System Administrator | Manage subscriptions, apps, and users within Content Innovation Cloud customer accounts. |
| **Studio Administration** | System Administrator | Administer and monitor Studio releases, applications, and process instances. |
| **Workspace** | Business User | Manage content-based processes, navigate the Hyland Content repository in a modern UI, and work with documents. |
| **Knowledge Discovery** | Data Analyst | Search and retrieve information from your enterprise content using contextual AI agents. |
| **Credentials** | Business User, System Administrator | Design, issue, anchor, and deliver verifiable credentials using the Hyland Credentials framework. |
| **Hyland for Workday** | Business User, System Administrator | Capture, view, and process Hyland content directly from within your Workday application. |
