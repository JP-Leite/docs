The Content Innovation Cloud (CIC) is designed to support various roles across an organisation, from day-to-day document management to advanced solution development.

## Business Users & Administrators

These roles focus on managing the platform, configuring business logic, and performing daily content operations.

### Business Administrators
**Goal**: Manage the organisation's overall CIC account and service subscriptions.
- **Key Tool**: **Administration Portal**
- **Responsibilities**: User onboarding, application provisioning, and subscription management.

### System Administrators
**Goal**: Monitor the health and performance of deployed applications and processes.
- **Key Tool**: **Studio Administration**
- **Responsibilities**: Monitoring process instances, managing task queues, and auditing system events.

### Business Users
**Goal**: Work with documents and complete content-driven tasks.
- **Key Tool**: **Workspace**, **Hyland for Workday**
- **Responsibilities**: Uploading files, metadata entry, searching for content, and completing workflow tasks.

### Data Analysts
**Goal**: Extract insights and answers from the organisation's vast content repository.
- **Key Tool**: **Knowledge Discovery**
- **Responsibilities**: Using AI agents to reason over documents and answer business-critical questions.

---

## Developers & Solution Builders

These roles leverage the CIC platform to build custom experiences and automate complex business processes.

### Low-Code/No-Code Builders
**Goal**: Rapidly develop process-driven applications without extensive coding.
- **Key Tool**: **Studio Modeler**
- **Responsibilities**: Visually modelling workflows, designing forms, and releasing application updates.

### Software Developers
**Goal**: Build fully custom applications and integrations.
- **Key Tool**: **Content REST API**, **Credentials REST API**
- **Responsibilities**: Programmatically interacting with the repository, managing metadata, and integrating CIC with existing enterprise systems.
