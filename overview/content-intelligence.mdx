## Overview

Content Intelligence adds AI capabilities to CIC, enabling automated document understanding, knowledge extraction, and intelligent agents.

## Products

<Columns cols={2}>
  <Card title="Agent Builder" icon="robot" href="/guides/agent-builder">
    Build and deploy AI agents with RAG, tool use, and task orchestration. Supports multiple LLMs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Discovery" icon="magnifying-glass-chart" href="/guides/knowledge-discovery">
    Semantic search and Q&A over your document corpus. Embed documents, ask questions, get cited answers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Knowledge Enrichment" icon="wand-magic-sparkles" href="/guides/knowledge-enrichment">
    Process documents through AI pipelines — extraction, classification, summarization, embeddings.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Intelligent Document Processing" icon="file-lines" href="/guides/idp">

  </Card>
