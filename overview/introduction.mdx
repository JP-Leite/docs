## What is CIC?

**Content Innovation Cloud (CIC)** is Hyland's cloud-native platform designed to bridge the gap between content management and process automation, supercharged by AI.

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Process Intelligence" icon="gears">
    Design and execute BPMN processes, DMN business rules, and dynamic forms.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content Services" icon="folder-tree">
    Store, version, and manage content with advanced search and security.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Content Intelligence" icon="sparkles">
    AI-powered document processing, agent building, and knowledge enrichment.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Platform Pillars

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Process & Automation">
    CIC uses a modern BPMN 2.0 engine to handle complex workflows. You can model your business logic in **Studio** and execute it in **Runtime**.
    
    - **Modeling Service**: Version-controlled BPMN and DMN models.
    - **Runtime Bundle**: High-performance process execution.
    - **Query Service**: Specialized search for process and task data.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Content & Repository">
    A scalable, cloud-native content store providing a centralized repository for all application documents and metadata.
    
    - **Repository API**: Standard REST operations for content lifecycle.
    - **Capture**: Automated document ingestion and classification.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="AI & Intelligence">
    Seamlessly integrate AI into your content workflows.
    
    - **Agent Builder**: Create custom AI agents for your business data.
    - **IDP**: Extract structured data from unstructured documents.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
