While most content repositories can store metadata and relationships, providing high-quality, organizing consistent metadata is challenging. Not only it requires a sufficient level of skills, but also enough time to complete.

AI helps by:

-
Automatically categorizing documents.

-
Generating summaries or descriptions.

-
Identifying named entities and relationships.
