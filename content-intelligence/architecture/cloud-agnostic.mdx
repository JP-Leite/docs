Although Hyland Cloud is the default environment, Content Intelligence is designed to support hybrid deployment.

We standardize on Kubernetes:

-
Services to be in line with K8s Services

-
Control plane and scaling to be K8s-native

This aligns with hyperscalers (AWS/EKS, Azure/AKS, GCP/GKE) and on-prem environments (e.g., OpenShift).
