Content Intelligence provides a comprehensive framework for building and deploying AI agents that can reason over your documents, answer complex questions, and automate content-driven decisions.

## The Agent Subsystem

The Agent subsystem is the foundation for creating intelligent, goal-oriented behaviors on top of your content.

### Agent Builder & Frameworks
- **Agent Builder Studio**: A low-code environment for designing agent personas, behaviors, and knowledge sources.
- **Agent Blueprints**: Pre-defined templates and APIs for rapidly creating agents tailored to specific business use cases.
- **Advanced Workflows**: Support for agentic chains, memory management, and human-in-the-loop feedback loops.

---

## Knowledge Enrichment & Context

Before an agent can effectively "know" your content, the **Context** subsystem enriches documents with additional intelligence.

- **Entity Recognition**: Identifying and linking key people, organizations, and locations.
- **Automatic Redaction**: Detecting and masking sensitive PII (Personally Identifiable Information).
- **Content Descriptions**: Generating AI-powered summaries for text and descriptive captions for images and audio.
- **Classification**: Automatically tagging content within pre-defined business ontologies.

---

## Knowledge Discovery

Knowledge Discovery is the primary user interface for interacting with your AI-enabled content.

- **Chat-to-Document**: Users can ask questions in natural language and receive answers synthesized directly from their repository.
- **Source Grounding**: Every answer provided by an agent is "grounded" in your content. Users can click linked citations to see the exact source excerpts used to generate the response, eliminating hallucinations and ensuring transparency.
- **Complex Semantic Queries**: Beyond simple keyword search, users can find content related to a topic or meaning, even if specific keywords are not present.

---

## Agent Types & Capabilities

CIC supports a variety of agent architectures to meet diverse requirements:

- **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**: Agents that retrieve relevant document chunks before generating a response to ensure accuracy.
- **ReAct / Tool-Based**: Agents capable of performing actions, such as looking up data in an external system or triggering a workflow.
- **Multi-modal Agents**: Intelligent systems that can reason across text, images, and structured data simultaneously.

## Assist & Automate

Once agents are grounded in your content, they move beyond simple Q&A to active process participation:
- **Decision Support**: Assisting human workers by summarizing case history or identifying missing evidence.
- **Full Automation**: Automating repetitive decisions within process workflows based on the agent's reasoning over document content.
