IDP supports the following languages:

- English
- Spanish
- German
- Italian
- French
- Portuguese
- Polish

To change the language preference:

1. On the Studio Modeler Home page, click the Settings icon. The Settings menu is displayed.
2. Point to Languages and select the relevant language in the list.
