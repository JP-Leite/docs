---
title: "Configuring Document Ai"
---

The IDP platform leverages AI and automation to streamline the extraction, classification, and processing of unstructured data. The platform incorporates state-of-the-art NLP (Natural Language Processing) and ML (Machine Learning) techniques to automatically classify, extract, and validate data from unstructured and semi-structured documents without heavy reliance on templates or manual configuration.

You can work with a wide range of document types such as, simple forms, invoices, contracts, financial statements, and handwritten notes.

The prompt-based extraction service enables you to define what data to extract by using prompts or queries.
