title: "Getting Started with IDP"
---

To begin using Hyland Intelligent Document Processing (IDP), you must first access the platform through the Experience User Portal and configure your workspace.

## Accessing the Application

The IDP interface is integrated within **Studio Modeler**.
- **URL**: [https://experience.hyland.com/](https://experience.hyland.com/)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Account Activation">
    You will receive an invitation email from your IT Administrator with an activation link.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Password Setup">
    Use the link to create a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="SSO Authentication">
    IDP supports authentication via **Microsoft Entra ID** or **Okta SAML IDP**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Studio Modeler">
    On the Home page, click **Studio Modeler** to enter the configuration environment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Capabilities & Localization

### Supported File Formats
IDP can process a wide variety of document types:
- **Images**: .JPG, .PNG, .TIFF
- **Documents**: .PDF, .DOC, .DOCX
- **Spreadsheets**: .XLS, .XLSX
- **Email**: .MSG, .EML

### Supported Languages
The platform interface and processing engine support:
- English, Spanish, German, Italian, French, Portuguese, and Polish.

**To change your language preference**:
1. On the Studio Modeler Home page, click the **Settings** icon.
2. Navigate to **Languages** and select your preferred language.
