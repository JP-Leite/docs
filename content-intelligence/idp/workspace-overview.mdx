---
title: "Workspace Overview"
---

Hyland Workspace is a content management application built with the Application Development Framework. Workspace enables you to start processes and perform your tasks as defined in the process workflow. You can also view tasks that are completed and tasks that are in queue. You can also see the processes that are running and completed.

You can view a repository of documents.

For more information, see the relevant section in the Hyland Workspace documentation: [Process Management](https://support.hyland.com/access?dita:topicPath=can1694758223311.dita).
