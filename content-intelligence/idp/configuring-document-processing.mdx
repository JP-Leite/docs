---
title: "Configuring Document Processing"
---

This section describes the workflow automation capabilities that enables you to build, customize, and automate end-to-end document workflows, from import to validation, extraction, and export.

You can configure workflows that include conditional logic to route documents, trigger actions based on specific criteria (For example, send high-value invoices to specific teams), or automate exceptions handling.

You can assign tasks to team members, share documents, and manage document approval processes across teams.

Through IDP platform's well documented API, you can easily integrate through HX Automate with a wide variety of business systems (e.g., ERP, CRM, Hyland RPA ) to enable seamless data flow between systems.

There are also pre-built connectors to popular enterprise tools (e.g., Microsoft 365, Salesforce, SAP, ServiceNow) that enable you to streamline integrations and reduce implementation time in Automate.

The processing limit for a single batch is 500 documents.
