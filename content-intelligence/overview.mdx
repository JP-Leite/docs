Content Intelligence is the AI layer of the Content Innovation Cloud. It transforms unstructured content into structured, actionable knowledge — enabling organisations to extract data, classify documents, build AI agents, and surface answers from their content at scale.

## What You Can Do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Build AI Agents" icon="robot">
    Use **Agent Builder** to create specialised AI agents that understand your content, answer business questions, and execute tasks autonomously. Agents can be configured via the Studio Modeler and launched directly from Workspace.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Automate Document Processing" icon="file-magnifying-glass">
    Use **IDP** to configure prompt-based document classification, field extraction workflows, and human-in-the-loop review processes powered by AI — without writing code.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Search and Synthesise Content" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Knowledge Discovery** lets users ask natural language questions against your enterprise content, with AI agents retrieving and synthesising answers from indexed repositories.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
