## Quick Start

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your Credentials">
    Acquire your Client ID and Client Secret from the HxP Admin Portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authenticate">
    Follow the [Authentication guide](/overview/authentication) to get your first Bearer token.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make your first call">
    Use the [Query Service API](/api/query) to list your current tasks or process instances.
  </Step>
</Steps>
